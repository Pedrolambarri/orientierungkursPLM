Top five food Contributions from Mex to the world

There is a wide range of foods that have their origins in pre-Hispanic Mexico specially in the Aztec Empire, do you know which ones they are?

Number One: CHOCOLATE

One of the most amazing and desired substances in the world, often addictive.

Number Two: VANILLA

It is the second most expensive species in the world.

Number Three: TOMATOES

Yes, tomatoes comes aslo from Mesoamerica, now days it's hard to find a kitchen in the world that does not use tomatoes

Number Four: AVOCADO

Trendy stuff now days, but Aztecas where eating avocado long long time ago. A curious fact is that the origin of the word Aguacate, comes from the Nahuatl "ahuacatl", which means "testicles of the tree"

Number Five: TEQUILA

History of Tequila it's amazing, born many years ago in a tiny town en Jalisco Mexico, Tequila it's now worldwide famous and well appreciated
