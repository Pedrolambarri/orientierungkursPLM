Rancho Beans

Ingredients

  • Dry Beans
  • Garlic
  • Oil, could be Olive or Canoil
  • Salt
  • Water

Cooking

  1. Put two cups of dry beans in a bowl with water and leave them the whole night untill the next morning.
  2. Take away all the water and wash theme with more mater a few times
  3. Ones you took away the water put the beans in a pot with 4 cups of water
  4. Be very patient and cook the beans, with the lowest heat possible for about 3 or 4 hours
  5. After the 4 hours check with a spoon if the beans are already very soft
  6. If the beans are already super soft, add one littl spoon of sea salt
  7. Add 5 spoons of Olive oil or Canoil and 3 garlic cloves
  8. After you added the salt, the garlic and the oil, leav the beans for another hour
  9. You will have amazing beans It's worth the wait and the time I promise!
  10. Make some tacos my friend
